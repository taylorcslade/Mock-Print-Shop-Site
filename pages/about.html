ABOUT ME
hello!

My name is Taylor and I am the owner of 'Taylor's Prints'. This shop is a way for me to combine two of my favorite things to do: traveling and photography.

I have travelled to some beautiful places and taking pictures is a way for me to remember the beauty of where I once was. All of these prints are photos taken by me with love, and adoration for the world around me! I hope you enjoy these prints and have a piece of international heaven in your home.
